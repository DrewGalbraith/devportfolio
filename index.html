About Me

Hey! My name is Drew Galbraith. I like to study large language models (LLMs) and machine learning generally. I studied linguistics as an undergrad because I was fascinated by the systems behind language and that researchers were studying them. That interest brought me to Natural Language Processing (NLP) and, more recently, to working with LLMs. I also like studying algorithms and finding ways to use them for things like solving games. Check out my blog and feel free to reach out!
